Welcome to Pune Guide

Explore Pune's Famous Places

  1. Shaniwar Wada – Historic fort built in 1732 and one of Pune’s most famous landmarks.
  2. Rajiv Gandhi Zoo – A popular zoo in Pune with many animals, birds and reptiles.
  3. Saras Baug – A peaceful garden with a famous Ganpati temple.
  4. Aga Khan Palace – A historical palace connected with Mahatma Gandhi.

Best Things to Do in Pune

  1. Visit historical forts and palaces
  2. Explore famous Pune street food
  3. Relax in beautiful gardens
  4. Visit museums and cultural places
  5. Enjoy shopping in local markets
